Naan and Tandoori Chicken

What is Naan and Tandoori Chicken

The dish consists of two foods, one of them is Naan which is flatbread. The other one is Tandoori Chicken which is chicken that is prepared with the Tandoor spice. The dish can range from a little bit of flavor to tongue burning heat. Although it is very spciy that taste is amazing
